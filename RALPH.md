Before your start, read @plan.md completely. Ensure you have run `ish prime` in order to fully understand our issue tracking system.

Review the ready ishes and choose the best one to work on using your own judgment; do not just take the first ready item you see. There may be multiple ready candidates, so quickly assess them and pick the one that is the strongest next action for the project. 

Then set the ish to `in-progress` and complete its work. Your work is not considered completed until:

1. All validations pass
2. Anything that will be useful to future workers on this issue is recorded in the `plan.md`
3. Commit all changed files (including files you didn't specifically change) with a conventional commit message.
4. You have set the ish to `completed`.
